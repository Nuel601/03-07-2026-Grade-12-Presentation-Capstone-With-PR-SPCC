Barangay Clearance

Required for various transactions including employment, business permits, and legal matters. This document certifies your good standing in the community.

Processing: Same day
Requirements:
  • Valid Government-issued ID

Certificate of Residency

Proof of residency for school enrollment, loans, government transactions.

Processing: Same day
Requirements:
  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Proof of Residency (utility bill or barangay ID)
  • Completed application form

Certificate of Indigency

For government assistance programs and social welfare benefits.

Processing: 1–2 days
Requirements:
  • Valid ID
  • Proof of Income or Affidavit of No Income
  • Proof of Residency

Barangay ID

Official ID for local transactions and supplementary identification.

Processing: 3–5 working days
Requirements:
  • Valid ID (if available)
  • Proof of Residency
  • 1x1 ID photo
  • Completed application form
